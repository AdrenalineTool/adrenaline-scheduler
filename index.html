Venue/location — groups fields at the same physical site into pods. Teams never play at two different venues. Lot name — the parking lot for teams at that field. If a venue has multiple lots serving different field clusters, assign different lot names to those fields (e.g. Fields 1-3 → "Lot A", Fields 4-6 → "Lot B"). Teams get parking passes for their specific lot based on which fields they play on. Parking pass color — assign a unique color per lot for easy visual identification at check-in.
Parking lots (define lots, assign fields, set pass colors)
Each parking lot serves a cluster of fields. Define lots here — enter the lot name, total spaces, pass color, and which fields are served. The scheduler routes larger divisions to fields adjacent to larger lots. Teams get passes for their specific lot.

Snake draft distributes teams by strength tier. Drag teams between pools to override. Each pool should have representation across Elite / Strong / Mid / Lower.
Pool seeding method: (1) Sort by skill tier — Elite > Strong > Mid > Lower, random within tier. (2) Snake draft into pools. Day 2 standings criteria: Win=3pts, Tie=1pt, Loss=0 → Head-to-head → Fewest goals against → Goal differential.
